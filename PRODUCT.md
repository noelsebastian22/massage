# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Locals and Blue Mountains visitors (Blackheath NSW) seeking remedial, Swedish, sports, lymphatic, hot stone massage or facial treatments. They arrive stressed, in pain, or planning self-care during a mountains getaway. Job to be done: understand what's offered, trust the practitioner's qualifications, and book by text or call. Mostly mobile, often on holiday.

## Product Purpose

Single-page marketing site for Cloud 3 Massage Therapy — the practice of Gerhard Hassler, a qualified remedial massage therapist (AMT member, practising full time since 2015). Success = visitors feel the calm of the clinic before they arrive, and convert to a text/call booking (0421 582 509) or an enquiry form message.

## Brand Personality

Still, grounded, therapeutic. The interface should feel like the clinic's quiet garden setting: deep forest green, warm candle-lit photography, unhurried serif typography. Calm confidence, not luxury-spa gloss; genuine therapeutic care, not pampering clichés.

## Anti-references

- Generic day-spa template sites (pink lotus flowers, stock zen stones on white)
- Cramped, information-dense clinical sites
- Anything that deviates from the provided design reference (`design.webp`) — the layout, palette, and typographic register of that mock are the committed direction; content is adapted from cloud3massagetherapy.com.au

## Design Principles

1. **The mock is the contract** — duplicate `design.webp`'s structure, palette, and rhythm faithfully; adapt content, not layout.
2. **Stillness through pacing** — generous whitespace, slow easings, nothing bounces or rushes.
3. **One human, honestly presented** — the team section becomes a solo-practitioner section for Gerhard; real qualifications and clinic details build trust.
4. **Booking is a conversation** — every CTA leads to text/call or the enquiry form; no fake booking engines.
5. **Photography carries the warmth** — candle-lit, skin-tone-rich imagery; the UI stays quiet around it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast. All carousels operable by keyboard. `prefers-reduced-motion` honoured with crossfades/instant states. Semantic landmarks and alt text throughout. Phone numbers as `tel:`/`sms:` links.
